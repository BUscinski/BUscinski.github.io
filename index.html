Hybrid Engineer / Designer

I've spent over a decade at the frontier of human-computer interaction — turning vague concepts into functional prototypes that move teams forward. My background is in physics and engineering, but my superpower is the space between disciplines: I speak designer, engineer, and researcher fluently and I build the thing in the middle that proves the idea.

I'm currently based in Pompano Beach, FL and open to roles in XR design, HCI research, interactive simulation, and education technology.

Technology is most powerful when it brings people together, not when it competes for their attention. My work — from XR enterprise tools to social games to climate data visualization — is driven by one question: does this make the interaction between humans better?
